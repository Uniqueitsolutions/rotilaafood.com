Dry Bhakhari

Our classic Bhakhari is crafted using only the finest ingredients, including pure desi ghee, ensuring a rich and authentic taste that's true to its roots. Each bite is a symphony of flavors, with the golden crispiness complemented by the subtle richness of the ghee. Our commitment to quality shines through in every aspect of its preparation, from sourcing the freshest ingredients to the skilled hands that shape and fry each Bhakhari to perfection. Indulge in the pure essence of our heritage with every bite of our Bhakhari, a timeless delicacy made with love and tradition.
